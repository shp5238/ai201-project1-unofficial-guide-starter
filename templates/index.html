How to use

  1. Type a career question in the box at the bottom and press the send arrow (or hit Enter).
  2. The assistant answers only from official USD career documents and lists its sources.
  3. If your question isn't covered by the documents, it will tell you instead of guessing.
  4. Tap an example chip to try a sample question.
  5. Use the Clear chat button to start over, and the toggle (top-right) for light/dark mode.
